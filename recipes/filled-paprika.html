Filled Paprika!

Filled paprika, a timeless classic.

What you are going to need:

  • Bell peppers in the colour of choice (3).
  • Ground beef (400g)
  • Cheese (100g)
  • Corn (50g)
  • Tomatoes (2)
  • Spices (paprika, pepper, salt)
  • Rice (250g)
  • Onion (1)
  • Garlic

Now for the preparation:

  1. Preheat the oven at 200Â°C.
  2. Boil water for the rice.
  3. Cut the bell pepper in half and remove all seeds.
  4. Cut the onion and garlic
  5. Cook the onion and garlic in oil for 1 min.
  6. Add the ground beef and cook till brown
  7. Add spices.
  8. Add the tomatoes and corn. Let it cook for 5 more minutes.
  9. Put the mixture in the bell peppers and cover in cheese.
  10. Put the bell peppers in the oven. for 16 minutes
  11. Cook the rice for 10 minutes.

Enjoy !
