Pasta pesto!

Pasta pesto, a timeless classic done in less then 30 minutes.

The perfect dish for a nice summer day.

The blend of tomatoes, pesto and parmazaan cheese bring out a fresh flavour.

This dish is also perfectly servable cold, so any leftovers can be enjoyed the next day.

What you are going to need for the recipe (2 person):

  • Pasta, tagliatelli or penne is preferred (250g)
  • Sun dried tomatoes, cherrytomatoes also work
  • Pesto (100g)
  • Pinetree seeds (25g)
  • Parmazaan cheese (25g)
  • Chicken breast (200g)
  • Chicken spices

Now for the preparation:

  1. Start boiling 2l of water.
  2. Slice the chicken breast into cubes, spice them a little.
  3. Start baking the pine seeds in a small pan on low heat.
  4. Boil the pasta for based on the instructions.
  5. Prepare chicken in the pan. this will take around 6 minutes.
  6. When the pasta is done cooking, take some of the hot water used to boil it. Discard the rest.
  7. Mix the pesto 1:1 with hot water.
  8. Mix the pesto into the pasta.
  9. Serve with chicken, sprinkle with pine seeds and parmazaan.
  10. Bon appetite !
