Couscous salad!

Couscous salad, an exotic delight!

Don't feel like cooking on a hot day ?

You just want to enjoy something fresh yet healthy ?

sounds like a couscous salad is something for you. You will have to do practicly no cooking for amazing flavours.

What you are going to need (2 person size):

  • couscous (250g)
  • cherry tomatoes (125g)
  • mozerella cheese (125g)
  • basel leaves
  • balsamic vinegar
  • pepper and salt
  • avocados (2)

Now for the preperation:

  1. Boil 500ml of water and pour it over the couscous in a shallow dish.
  2. Cover the dish and let it sit for 5 minutes.
  3. Cut the tomatoes in half.
  4. Cut the leaves into small flakes.
  5. Cut the avocado into cubes.
  6. Cut the mozerella into cubes around the same size as the avocado.
  7. Mix it all in a bowl.
  8. Spice to flavour with salt, pepper and balsamic vinegar.

Enjoy !
